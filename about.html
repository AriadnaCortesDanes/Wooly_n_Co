The Pattern Library

The Pattern Library is an open source project aimed to allow everyone free access to crochet patters. It is also conveniently used as a personal pattern database. All patterns in this webside are collected from other free pattern websides, posts or are designed by us. Feel free to copy, share or suggest any improvement.


About Us

We are just a couple of young crochet amateurs, who also happen to study informatics. We started with our first projects in 2021, and we are learning since then. We are based in Barcelona, Spain.
